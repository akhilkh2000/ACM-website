ABOUT US...

The ACM Student Chapter here at PES University Electronic City Campus brings you a world of opportunities, events and contains a wide range of activities to keep ACM moving including organizing conferences, improving Technical skills, Networking, and we strive to provide a professional experience to our members.





We have introduced ACM student chapter into our college to draw out and encourage the young minds to explore the different domains in the technical world. Each individual is given the opportunity to portray their potential.
Awareness is raised regarding the important technical, educational and social issues around the world which keeps the professionals well informed.

Benefits Of Student membership

  • Learning Center with resources for lifelong learning, including online courses targeted toward essential IT skills and popular certifications
  • Subscription to the full ACM Digital Library, which includes over 2 million pages of text
  • Ability to search DL, access bibliographic citations, view article abstracts
  • A full year (12 issues) online subscription to Communications of the ACM
  • A full-year (4 issues) subscription to XRDS: Crossroads, ACM's Student Magazine
  • A full-year electronic subscription to monthly MemberNet newsletter
  • Student Quick Takes (ACM's quarterly newsletters)
  • TechNews (ACM's tri-weekly email IT news digest)
  • CareerNews, ACM's twice monthly email career news digest
  • Ubiquity, ACM's IT opinion magazine and forum, and eLearn, ACM's magazine dedicated to distance education
  • Discounts on subscriptions to ACM journals, magazines, books, and conferences. Exclusive subscription pricing on ACM Books series.
  • A free email forwarding address with high-quality spam filtering
  • Special discounts and access to valuable products and services through the ACM's Insurance and Discounts and Special Offers programs
  • Technical Interest Profile Alerts
  • Participation in ACM Distinguished Speakers Program (DSP)
